About

Raj Vikramaditya, popularly known as Striver, is a passionate software engineer, mentor, and educator who has become an icon in the coding community. Coming from a tier-3 college, he proved that with consistency, discipline, and a strong work ethic, one can reach the top without a prestigious background.

After working at companies like Media.net and Google, he chose to give back to the community by launching YouTube tutorials and the A2Z DSA Sheet — a structured and beginner-friendly guide to mastering Data Structures and Algorithms. His efforts have empowered lakhs of students across India and beyond to crack technical interviews.

Striver believes in creating impact, not just content. He emphasizes conceptual clarity, problem-solving ability, and self-discipline. His journey stands as motivation for countless students preparing for placements from remote or underrepresented institutions.
